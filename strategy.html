Strategy

Credit solutions for complex situations

ShoreVest provides asset-backed credit solutions to borrowers and financial institutions across China.

The approach draws on underwriting and resolution experience built in China since 2004.

STRATEGIES

A large and complex credit market

Why this persists

FINANCING NEED

Credit dislocation

Changes in traditional and non-bank lending have created financing and restructuring needs across vast parts of China’s credit market.

MARKET ACCESS

Limited familiarity

Many participants in global capital markets have limited experience with China’s regulatory, enforcement and creditor landscape, favoring platforms with local relationships and execution capabilities built over time.

EXECUTION

Asset-level complexity

Outcomes depend on documentation, control rights, asset-level diligence and the ability to execute through local processes.

Three strategies

One investment discipline

Deployment shifts with the opportunity set as market conditions and pricing change.

Common underwriting considerations

Margin of safety Entry terms are assessed against downside cases and expected recovery value.
Legal and structural position Claim priority, documentation and enforceability are reviewed for each situation.
Exit routes Potential repayment, restructuring and resolution routes are considered before capital is committed.

Risk management and downside analysis are applied across investments, at both the transaction and portfolio level.

Credit solutions & sourcing

The trusted provider of credit solutions

ShoreVest works with banks, asset-management companies, corporate borrowers, legal advisers and servicing counterparties across China.

  1. Financial institutions

    Banks and asset-management companies work with ShoreVest to finance, restructure and resolve credit, and are a primary source of opportunities across the platform.

  2. Corporate borrowers

    ShoreVest works with companies seeking asset-backed financing or restructuring solutions appropriate to their circumstances.

  3. Local presence

    Touchpoints across China’s major commercial centers, together with legal, servicing and asset-management capabilities, support diligence, structuring and implementation.
