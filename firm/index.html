Who we are

A China-focused private credit platform

Since 2004, the ShoreVest team has managed more than US$2 billion in private credit investments in China on behalf of institutional investors.

The platform has developed through relationships with borrowers, financial institutions, service providers and institutional capital partners, supported by local teams and institutional investment-management processes.
