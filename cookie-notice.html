This Cookie Notice applies to all users of the Site, including visitors to public pages and users accessing restricted or investor-only areas. Certain features of the Site may not function properly without the use of strictly necessary cookies.

2. What are cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. ShoreVest may also use local storage objects, web beacons, pixels, tracking tags, device identifiers and server-side logging mechanisms. For the purposes of this Notice, all such technologies are referred to collectively as cookies.

(a) Strictly necessary cookies

Essential for the operation, security and integrity of the Site, including page navigation, session management, fraud prevention and access control. No consent is required or sought.

Collect aggregated data on how users interact with the Site to improve functionality and experience. In jurisdictions requiring prior consent, including those subject to EU GDPR and UK GDPR, these cookies are deployed only after obtaining explicit consent. ShoreVest does not rely on legitimate interests as the basis for analytics cookies where prior consent is required.

(c) Preference and functionality cookies

Allow the Site to remember user choices such as language preferences and display settings. Deployed only with prior consent where required by applicable law.

(d) Security and access control cookies

Used in restricted or investor-access areas to authenticate users, maintain secure sessions, enforce access controls and detect suspicious activity. Deployed on the basis of legitimate interests in protecting the Site and its users.

(e) Third-party cookies

May be set by third-party service providers including analytics tools, hosting infrastructure, content delivery networks and cybersecurity services. ShoreVest does not control the independent practices of these providers. A summary of principal cookies in use is available on request from privacy@shorevest.com.

4. Duration

Cookies may be session cookies deleted on browser close, or persistent cookies remaining for a defined period. Retention periods vary by purpose and technical requirements.

5. Legal basis and consent

Strictly necessary cookies require no consent. Performance, analytics and preference cookies are deployed only where required legal standards are met; in jurisdictions requiring prior consent, these are deployed only after explicit consent via the cookie consent interface.
