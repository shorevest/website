# Build a Career in Complex Credit

ShoreVest is a specialist private credit firm focused on complex investment opportunities in China.

Our people are central to the firm’s success. We recruit selectively, invest in long-term development, and seek professionals with the intellectual ability, judgment, and discipline required to perform at a high level.

A career at ShoreVest offers exposure to situations that often sit beyond the scope of conventional lending. Our professionals work across financial analysis, collateral assessment, legal rights, restructuring, and recovery strategy to identify opportunities, manage risk, and protect value.

**View Open Positions**

## Developing Versatile Investors

Our goal is to develop professionals who can assess an investment from multiple perspectives and follow it through its full life cycle.

This requires more than technical proficiency. Strong investors must be able to develop an independent view, understand the interests of different stakeholders, evaluate downside protection, and determine how value can be preserved or recovered when circumstances change.

Over time, investment professionals may gain experience across:

- Private credit and special situations
- Asset-backed lending
- Financial and commercial due diligence
- Collateral and recovery analysis
- Transaction structuring and documentation
- Portfolio monitoring
- Restructuring and debt resolution
- Investment execution and realization

The breadth of our work calls for intellectual curiosity, rigorous analysis, clear communication, and practical judgment.

## A Focused Firm

ShoreVest maintains a focused team across its offices in China.

Our size allows professionals to work closely with senior colleagues and contribute to substantive matters. Team members are expected to understand the broader context of their work, form their own views, and take responsibility for the quality of their analysis and execution.

We value people who are thoughtful, precise, and comfortable working through complexity. We also value those who can challenge assumptions constructively and communicate conclusions clearly.

Responsibility is earned through consistent performance.

## Investment Professionals

We look for professionals who want to develop deep expertise in private credit and complex investment situations.

Successful candidates are typically able to combine financial analysis with commercial awareness and practical judgment. They should be comfortable working with incomplete information, testing competing interpretations, and reaching defensible conclusions.

Depending on experience and role, investment professionals may contribute to:

- Identifying and evaluating investment opportunities
- Assessing companies, industries, assets, and capital structures
- Analyzing liquidity, leverage, and repayment capacity
- Evaluating collateral and downside protection
- Reviewing legal and transaction structures
- Supporting negotiations and execution
- Monitoring investments and changing risk factors
- Assessing restructuring and recovery options
- Presenting recommendations to senior decision-makers

We recruit only when we believe a candidate can strengthen the team.

## Business and Operating Functions

Our investment activities are supported by professionals across legal, finance, risk, operations, compliance, human resources, and client solutions.

These functions play an important role in the firm’s investment process, governance, operations, and relationships with investors and counterparties.

We seek professionals who bring relevant expertise, sound judgment, attention to detail, and a strong sense of accountability.

## Early-Career Opportunities

ShoreVest periodically recruits outstanding students and early-career professionals.

These roles provide direct exposure to the work involved in private credit and special situations. Early-career professionals are expected to contribute to live matters, develop quickly, and meet the same standards of accuracy, preparation, and professionalism expected across the firm.

We look for candidates with strong analytical ability, intellectual curiosity, clear communication skills, and a genuine interest in credit and investing.

## Experienced Professionals

We selectively recruit experienced professionals who can bring established expertise and independent judgment to the firm.

Candidates should be able to demonstrate a record of high-quality work, the ability to operate effectively in complex situations, and the maturity to take ownership of important responsibilities.

We are interested in substance rather than title. What matters is the quality of a candidate’s experience, thinking, and execution.

## Professional Development

Development at ShoreVest is grounded in substantive work, close collaboration, and direct feedback.

Professionals build their capabilities by working across different stages of the investment process, engaging with experienced colleagues, and taking on greater responsibility as their judgment develops.

The objective is not simply to build technical knowledge, but to develop professionals who can analyze complexity, make sound decisions, and contribute meaningfully over the long term.

## What We Value

### Intellectual Rigor

We examine the facts, test assumptions, and focus on the factors that determine risk and value.

### Judgment

We expect professionals to move beyond analysis and form clear, defensible views.

### Ownership

People are accountable for the quality, accuracy, and reliability of their work.

### Collaboration

Complex situations require different forms of expertise. We work closely across disciplines and value constructive challenge.

### Integrity

Professional integrity is fundamental to how we work with investors, counterparties, and one another.

ShoreVest offers careers for people who want to work on demanding investment situations, develop specialist expertise, and assume meaningful responsibility.

## Equal Employment Opportunity

ShoreVest is committed to providing equal employment opportunities in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

Employment decisions are based on qualifications, experience, performance, and the requirements of the role, without regard to any characteristic protected by applicable law.

## Recruitment Agencies

Recruitment agencies and search firms must obtain prior written approval from ShoreVest’s Human Resources team and agree to applicable terms before submitting candidate information.

ShoreVest does not accept responsibility for unsolicited submissions or related fees where no prior written agreement exists.

## Recruitment Fraud Notice

Candidates should remain alert to fraudulent emails, messages, or social media communications claiming to represent ShoreVest.

Official recruitment communications will take place through recognized ShoreVest channels. ShoreVest will never request payment from a candidate as part of the recruitment process.

Candidates should not provide financial information, passwords, or sensitive identity documents in response to unverified communications. Suspicious messages should be reported through the contact information published on ShoreVest’s official website.

## 培养全面的投资人才

我们的目标是培养能够从多个角度审视投资机会，并参与项目完整生命周期的专业投资人才。

这不仅需要扎实的专业能力。优秀的投资人还应能够形成独立观点，理解不同利益相关方的诉求，评估下行保护，并在情况发生变化时判断如何维护或实现价值回收。

随着经验的积累，投资人员可能接触以下领域：

- 私募信贷与特殊机会投资
- 资产支持型贷款
- 财务及商业尽职调查
- 抵押物与资产回收分析
- 交易结构设计与法律文件审阅
- 投后管理与风险监控
- 债务重组与债务处置
- 投资执行与退出回收

我们的工作要求团队成员具备求知欲、严谨的分析能力、清晰的沟通能力和务实的判断力。

## 专注而紧密的团队

新岸资本在中国各地设有办公室，并始终保持精干而专注的团队架构。

这样的组织模式使专业人员能够与资深同事密切合作，参与具有实质意义的项目，并深入理解投资决策背后的整体逻辑。团队成员不仅需要完成具体工作，还应理解项目所处的更广泛背景，形成独立观点，并对自身分析和执行的质量负责。

我们重视思考严密、工作精准，并能够从容处理复杂问题的人才。我们同样看重能够以建设性的方式质疑假设，并清晰表达结论的专业人员。

更大的责任来自持续而可靠的表现。

## 投资岗位

我们寻找希望在私募信贷和复杂投资机会领域建立深厚专业能力的人才。

优秀的候选人通常能够将财务分析、商业意识和务实判断相结合。他们应能够在信息不完整的情况下有效工作，检验不同观点，并形成有充分依据的结论。

根据岗位和经验水平，投资人员可能参与：

- 发掘及评估投资机会
- 分析企业、行业、资产及资本结构
- 评估流动性、杠杆水平和偿债能力
- 分析抵押物和下行保护
- 审阅法律及交易结构
- 支持交易谈判与执行
- 监控投资项目及不断变化的风险因素
- 评估重组及资产回收方案
- 向资深决策人员提出投资建议

只有在我们确信候选人能够增强团队整体实力时，才会作出聘用决定。

## 业务与运营岗位

公司的投资活动由法律、财务、风险管理、运营、合规、人力资源和客户解决方案等职能团队共同支持。

这些职能在公司的投资流程、治理体系、日常运营，以及与投资人和交易对手的关系中发挥着重要作用。

我们寻求具备相关专业能力、良好判断力、严谨细致的工作态度和高度责任感的人才。

## 学生及初级人才机会

新岸资本会根据业务需要，择优招聘表现出色的学生和初级专业人才。

这些岗位能够让候选人直接接触私募信贷和特殊机会投资的实际工作。初级团队成员需要参与真实项目，快速提升能力，并达到公司对准确性、充分准备和专业素养的一贯要求。

我们重视具备优秀分析能力、强烈求知欲、清晰沟通能力，并对信贷和投资具有真正兴趣的候选人。

## 资深专业人才

我们会审慎招聘能够为公司带来成熟专业能力和独立判断力的资深人才。

候选人应能够证明其过往工作质量，具备处理复杂情况的能力，并有足够的专业成熟度承担重要职责。

我们关注实际能力，而非职位头衔。真正重要的是候选人所积累的经验、思考问题的方式，以及将判断转化为有效执行的能力。

## 职业发展

新岸资本的人才发展建立在实质性工作、紧密协作和直接反馈的基础上。

专业人员通过参与投资流程的不同阶段、与经验丰富的同事合作，并随着判断力的提升承担更大责任，不断增强自身能力。

我们的目标不仅是帮助团队成员积累专业知识，更是培养能够分析复杂问题、作出合理决策，并长期为公司创造价值的专业人才。

## 我们所重视的品质

### 严谨分析

我们以事实为基础，检验关键假设，并聚焦真正决定风险和价值的核心因素。

### 专业判断

分析本身并不是终点。我们要求专业人员在充分分析的基础上，形成清晰且有依据的观点。

### 主人翁意识

每位团队成员都应对自身工作的质量、准确性和可靠性负责。

### 团队协作

复杂投资项目需要不同领域的专业能力。我们鼓励跨团队协作，并重视有建设性的专业讨论。

### 诚信

诚信是我们与投资人、交易对手及同事开展一切工作的基本原则。
