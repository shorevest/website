Our Approach

How We Work

Private credit depends on more than investment analysis. It requires sound investment judgment, effective execution, careful asset management, legal and risk oversight, accurate financial reporting, disciplined fund operations, and strong institutional relationships.

People at ShoreVest develop deep expertise in their own fields while understanding how their work connects to the wider business. Each role contributes differently, but the underlying requirement is the same: work must withstand challenge from colleagues, investment committees, counterparties, and, ultimately, the test of results.

Our Organization

The Firm

ShoreVest operates through a focused team across Guangzhou, Shanghai, Beijing, and Hong Kong. This structure allows team members to work closely with senior colleagues, understand the broader context of their responsibilities, and make substantive contributions from an early stage.

We expect people to reach clear judgments, defend them under scrutiny, and stand behind the quality of their work. Responsibility follows demonstrated judgment and execution, not simply time served.

Teams & Functions

Career Paths

ShoreVest hires across its investment platform and the functions required to manage investments, serve investors, control risk, and operate the firm effectively. Responsibilities differ by team and level, but each role has a direct bearing on the quality of the firm’s work.

01/07

Investment

Investment professionals identify and assess opportunities, analyze businesses and capital structures, conduct due diligence, and evaluate transaction and legal structures. They remain involved throughout the investment lifecycle, including portfolio monitoring, restructuring, recovery analysis, and the preparation of recommendations for the Investment Committee.

Capital Solutions

Capital Solutions forms part of the investment platform and develops and executes credit solutions involving borrowers, financial institutions, asset sellers, and other counterparties.

Asset Solutions

Asset Solutions teams monitor borrowers, assets, and collateral; support borrower engagement and workout processes; and coordinate with investment, legal, and external parties on restructuring, recovery, and enforcement.

Legal, Risk & Compliance

Legal, risk, and compliance teams review transaction structures and documentation, assess legal, regulatory, and investment risks, support due diligence and Investment Committee processes, and advise on governance and compliance matters.

Finance & Fund Operations

Finance and fund operations teams are responsible for the financial accuracy and operational reliability of the firm and its funds. Their work includes fund accounting, investor reporting, capital calls and distributions, audit, valuation, tax, and day-to-day financial operations.

Client Solutions

Client Solutions leads ShoreVest’s relationships with institutional investors across Asia-Pacific, the Americas, and EMEA. The team is responsible for fundraising, investor due diligence, communication, reporting, and ongoing institutional coverage.

Human Resources & Firm Operations

Human resources and firm operations teams build and support ShoreVest’s organization and maintain the infrastructure required for the firm to operate effectively. Their responsibilities include recruitment, compensation and benefits, performance management, training, workplace administration, and organizational planning.

Recruitment Notices

Equal Employment Opportunity

ShoreVest is committed to providing equal employment opportunities in accordance with applicable laws and regulations. Employment decisions are based on qualifications, experience, performance, and role requirements, without regard to any characteristic protected by applicable law.

Recruitment Agencies

Recruitment agencies and search firms must obtain prior written approval from ShoreVest’s Human Resources team and agree to the applicable terms before submitting candidate information. ShoreVest does not accept responsibility for unsolicited submissions or related fees when no prior written agreement exists.

Recruitment Fraud Notice

Candidates should remain alert to fraudulent emails, messages, or social media communications claiming to represent ShoreVest. Official recruiting communications take place only through recognized ShoreVest channels. ShoreVest will never ask a candidate to make a payment as part of the recruitment process. Candidates should not provide financial information, passwords, or sensitive identity documents in response to unverified communications and should report suspicious messages through the contact information on ShoreVest’s official website.
