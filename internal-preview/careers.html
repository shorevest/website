How We Work

Specialists Who Understand the Whole

Private credit depends on more than investment analysis. It requires investment judgment, effective transaction execution, careful asset management, legal and risk control, accurate financial reporting, disciplined fund operations, strong institutional relationships, and the effective management of the firm itself.

Professionals at ShoreVest develop expertise in their own fields while understanding how their work connects to the wider business. The nature of that contribution differs by role, but the standard does not: work should be accurate, well considered, and dependable.

Work Across ShoreVest

  • Investment analysis and transaction execution
  • Capital solutions
  • Asset servicing and collateral management
  • Portfolio monitoring
  • Asset restructuring and debt resolution
  • Legal, compliance, and risk control
  • Finance, valuation, and fund operations
  • Institutional client relationships
  • Human resources and firm administration

The Firm

A Focused Firm

ShoreVest operates with a focused team across Guangzhou, Shanghai, Beijing, and Hong Kong. Our size allows professionals to work closely with senior colleagues, understand the broader context of their responsibilities, and make substantive contributions from an early stage.

We expect team members to form considered views, communicate clearly, and take responsibility for the accuracy and reliability of their work.

Responsibility is earned through consistent performance.

Career Paths

Careers Across the Firm

ShoreVest recruits for different forms of expertise. Responsibilities vary by team and level, but every function contributes to the firm’s ability to evaluate opportunities, manage investments, serve investors, control risk, and operate effectively.

Investment and Capital Solutions

Investment professionals assess opportunities and help manage investments throughout their lifecycle. Depending on their experience and role, their work may include:

  • Identifying and evaluating investment opportunities
  • Analyzing companies, industries, assets, and capital structures
  • Conducting financial and commercial due diligence
  • Assessing liquidity, leverage, repayment capacity, collateral, and downside protection
  • Reviewing transaction and legal structures
  • Supporting negotiations, documentation, and execution
  • Monitoring investments and changing risk factors
  • Evaluating restructuring and recovery options
  • Presenting analysis and recommendations to senior colleagues and investment committees

Capital Solutions sits within ShoreVest’s investment platform and focuses on developing and executing solutions for borrowers, financial institutions, and other counterparties.

ShoreVest Asset Solutions

ShoreVest Asset Solutions professionals work on the servicing, control, and recovery of assets. Depending on the role, responsibilities may include:

  • Monitoring borrowers, assets, collateral, and agreed obligations
  • Coordinating asset servicing and collateral management
  • Supporting borrower engagement and workout processes
  • Working with legal, investment, and external parties on restructuring and enforcement matters
  • Tracking recovery progress and material changes in risk
  • Supporting the execution of debt-resolution strategies

Legal, Risk and Compliance

Legal, risk, and compliance professionals help ensure that transactions and firm activities are properly reviewed, documented, controlled, and conducted in accordance with applicable requirements. Their work may include:

  • Reviewing transaction structures and documentation
  • Assessing legal, regulatory, market, and credit risks
  • Supporting due diligence and investment committee processes
  • Developing and monitoring risk controls
  • Advising on governance, compliance, and regulatory matters
  • Coordinating with investment teams, counterparties, advisers, and service providers

Finance and Fund Operations

Finance and fund operations professionals are responsible for the financial accuracy and operational reliability of the firm and its funds. Their work may include:

  • Fund and management-company accounting
  • Financial and investor reporting
  • Budgeting and financial control
  • Capital calls, distributions, and cash management
  • Audit and tax coordination
  • Valuation processes and supporting analysis
  • Maintaining accurate records and operational controls
  • Coordinating with administrators, auditors, advisers, and other service providers

Client Solutions

Client Solutions manages ShoreVest’s relationships with institutional investors across Asia-Pacific, the Americas, and EMEA. Depending on the role, responsibilities may include:

  • Developing and maintaining institutional investor relationships
  • Preparing investor materials and responding to due diligence requests
  • Coordinating meetings, reporting, and ongoing communications
  • Supporting fundraising and investor coverage
  • Working with investment, finance, legal, and operations teams to provide accurate information
  • Monitoring relevant market and investor developments

Human Resources and Firm Operations

Human resources and firm-operations professionals help ShoreVest recruit, develop, and manage its people while maintaining the systems and administration required to operate effectively. Their work may include:

  • Recruitment and onboarding
  • Compensation, benefits, and performance processes
  • Training and professional development
  • Employee relations and organizational planning
  • Office administration and internal coordination
  • Maintaining reliable policies, records, and operating processes

Starting a Career at ShoreVest

Early-Career Opportunities

When opportunities arise, ShoreVest recruits strong students and early-career professionals across different areas of the firm.

Depending on the role, early-career professionals may contribute to investment analysis, live transactions, asset management, legal and risk work, finance, fund operations, client activity, or firm administration. They are expected to meet the same standards of accuracy, preparation, discretion, and professionalism as the rest of the firm.

We look for strong analytical or professional ability, intellectual curiosity, clear communication, and a genuine interest in the relevant field and in how a private credit firm operates.

Experience and Independent Judgment

Experienced Professionals

We selectively recruit experienced professionals who bring established expertise and independent judgment.

Candidates should demonstrate a record of high-quality work, the ability to operate effectively in complex and changing circumstances, and the maturity to take ownership of important responsibilities.

We assess candidates on the substance of their experience, judgment, and execution rather than title alone.

What We Value

What We Value

Rigor

We examine the facts, test assumptions, and focus on the matters that determine risk, value, and effective execution.

Judgment

Recruitment Notices

Equal Employment Opportunity

ShoreVest is committed to providing equal employment opportunities in accordance with applicable laws and regulations. Employment decisions are based on qualifications, experience, performance, and role requirements, without regard to any characteristic protected by applicable law.

Recruitment Agencies

Recruitment agencies and search firms must obtain prior written approval from ShoreVest’s Human Resources team and agree to the applicable terms before submitting candidate information. ShoreVest does not accept responsibility for unsolicited submissions or related fees when no prior written agreement exists.

Recruitment Fraud Notice

Candidates should remain alert to fraudulent emails, messages, or social media communications claiming to represent ShoreVest. Official recruiting communications take place only through recognized ShoreVest channels. ShoreVest will never ask a candidate to make a payment as part of the recruitment process. Candidates should not provide financial information, passwords, or sensitive identity documents in response to unverified communications and should report suspicious messages through the contact information on ShoreVest’s official website.
