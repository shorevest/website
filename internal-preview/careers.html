Investment Development

Developing Versatile Investors

We develop professionals who can assess credit investments from multiple angles and follow them throughout the investment lifecycle. That requires more than technical proficiency. It requires independent thinking, an understanding of competing stakeholder interests, a clear view of downside protection, and the judgment to determine how risk and value should be managed as circumstances change.

Experience Across the Investment Lifecycle

  • Private credit investing in China
  • Financial and commercial due diligence
  • Asset-backed lending
  • Transaction structuring and documentation
  • Collateral and downside analysis
  • Portfolio monitoring
  • Asset restructuring and debt resolution
  • Investment execution and realization

The Firm

A Focused Firm

ShoreVest operates with a focused team across Guangzhou, Shanghai, Beijing, and Hong Kong. Our size means that professionals work closely with senior colleagues and contribute to substantive work from an early stage. We expect team members to understand the broader context of their work, form independent views, and take responsibility for the quality of their analysis and execution.

Responsibility is earned through consistent performance.

Career Paths

Investment and Business Careers

Investment Professionals

We look for professionals who want to develop deep expertise in private credit investing. They should be comfortable working with incomplete information, testing competing interpretations, and reaching clear, defensible conclusions.

Depending on their experience and role, investment professionals may contribute to:

Role Contribution

  • Identifying and evaluating investment opportunities
  • Analyzing companies, industries, assets, and capital structures
  • Assessing liquidity, leverage, and repayment capacity
  • Evaluating collateral and downside protection
  • Reviewing legal and transaction structures
  • Supporting negotiations and transaction execution
  • Monitoring investments and changing risk factors
  • Assessing restructuring and recovery options
  • Presenting recommendations to senior decision-makers

Business and Operating Functions

ShoreVest’s investment activities are supported by professionals across legal, finance, risk, operations, compliance, human resources, and client solutions. These functions are integral to the firm’s investment process, governance, and relationships with investors and counterparties.

Early-Career Opportunities

When opportunities arise, ShoreVest recruits strong students and early-career professionals for roles with direct exposure to private credit investing. Early-career professionals may contribute to live investments and transactions from the outset and are held to the same standards of accuracy, preparation, and professionalism as the rest of the firm.

We look for strong analytical ability, intellectual curiosity, clear communication, and a genuine interest in credit and investing.

Experienced Professionals

We selectively recruit experienced professionals who bring established expertise and independent judgment. Candidates should demonstrate a record of high-quality work, the ability to operate effectively across complex transactions and changing circumstances, and the maturity to take ownership of important responsibilities.

We assess candidates on the substance of their experience, judgment, and execution, rather than title alone.

What We Value

What We Value

Intellectual Rigor

We examine the facts, test assumptions, and focus on what determines risk and value.

Judgment

We expect professionals to move beyond analysis and form clear, defensible views.

Ownership

We take responsibility for the quality, accuracy, and reliability of our work.

Collaboration

Strong investment decisions draw on different forms of expertise. We work closely across disciplines and value constructive challenge.

Integrity

Professional integrity is fundamental to how we work with investors, counterparties, and one another.

Recruitment Notices

Equal Employment Opportunity

ShoreVest is committed to providing equal employment opportunities in accordance with applicable laws and regulations. Employment decisions are based on qualifications, experience, performance, and role requirements, without regard to any characteristic protected by applicable law.

Recruitment Agencies

Recruitment agencies and search firms must obtain prior written approval from ShoreVest’s Human Resources team and agree to the applicable terms before submitting candidate information. ShoreVest does not accept responsibility for unsolicited submissions or related fees when no prior written agreement exists.

Recruitment Fraud Notice

Candidates should remain alert to fraudulent emails, messages, or social media communications claiming to represent ShoreVest. Official recruiting communications take place only through recognized ShoreVest channels. ShoreVest will never ask a candidate to make a payment as part of the recruitment process. Candidates should not provide financial information, passwords, or sensitive identity documents in response to unverified communications and should report suspicious messages through the contact information on ShoreVest’s official website.
