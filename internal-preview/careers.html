Build a Career in Complex Credit

ShoreVest is a specialist private credit firm focused on complex investment opportunities in China.

A career here means working on situations that sit beyond the scope of conventional lending: financial analysis, collateral assessment, legal rights, restructuring, and recovery strategy, all in service of identifying opportunities, managing risk, and protecting value.

Investment development

Developing Versatile Investors

We develop professionals who can assess an investment from multiple angles and follow it through its full life cycle. That takes more than technical proficiency. It requires an independent view, an understanding of competing stakeholder interests, a clear read on downside protection, and the judgment to know how value is preserved or recovered when circumstances change.

Experience over time

  • Private credit and special situations
  • Asset-backed lending
  • Financial and commercial due diligence
  • Collateral and recovery analysis
  • Transaction structuring and documentation
  • Portfolio monitoring
  • Restructuring and debt resolution
  • Investment execution and realization

This work rewards intellectual curiosity, rigorous analysis, clear communication, and practical judgment.

The firm

A Focused Firm

ShoreVest maintains a focused team across its offices in China. Our size means professionals work closely with senior colleagues on substantive matters from early on. We expect team members to understand the broader context of their work, form their own views, and take responsibility for the quality of their analysis and execution.

We value people who are thoughtful, precise, and comfortable with complexity, and who can challenge assumptions constructively and communicate conclusions clearly.

Responsibility is earned through consistent performance.

Investment careers

Investment Professionals

We look for professionals who want to build deep expertise in private credit and complex investment situations: people comfortable working with incomplete information, testing competing interpretations, and reaching defensible conclusions.

Depending on experience and role, investment professionals may contribute to:

Role contribution

  • Identifying and evaluating investment opportunities
  • Assessing companies, industries, assets, and capital structures
  • Analyzing liquidity, leverage, and repayment capacity
  • Evaluating collateral and downside protection
  • Reviewing legal and transaction structures
  • Supporting negotiations and execution
  • Monitoring investments and changing risk factors
  • Assessing restructuring and recovery options
  • Presenting recommendations to senior decision-makers

We recruit only when we believe a candidate can strengthen the team.

Business and Operating Functions

Our investment activities are supported by professionals across legal, finance, risk, operations, compliance, human resources, and client solutions, functions central to the firm's investment process, governance, and relationships with investors and counterparties.

We seek professionals who bring relevant expertise, sound judgment, attention to detail, and a strong sense of accountability.

Early-Career Opportunities

ShoreVest periodically recruits outstanding students and early-career professionals into roles with direct exposure to private credit and special situations work. Early-career professionals contribute to live matters from the outset and are held to the same standards of accuracy, preparation, and professionalism as the rest of the firm.

We look for strong analytical ability, intellectual curiosity, clear communication, and a genuine interest in credit and investing.

Experienced Professionals

We selectively recruit experienced professionals who bring established expertise and independent judgment. Candidates should be able to demonstrate a record of high-quality work, the ability to operate effectively in complex situations, and the maturity to own important responsibilities.

We are interested in substance rather than title. What matters is the quality of a candidate's experience, thinking, and execution.

Professional Development

Development at ShoreVest is grounded in substantive work, close collaboration, and direct feedback. Professionals build capability by working across different stages of the investment process, engaging with experienced colleagues, and taking on greater responsibility as their judgment develops.

The goal is not simply technical knowledge, but professionals who can analyze complexity, make sound decisions, and contribute meaningfully over the long term.

What we value

What We Value

Intellectual Rigor

We examine the facts, test assumptions, and focus on what determines risk and value.

Judgment

We expect professionals to move beyond analysis and form clear, defensible views.

Ownership

People are accountable for the quality, accuracy, and reliability of their work.

Collaboration

Integrity

Equal Employment Opportunity

ShoreVest is committed to providing equal employment opportunities in accordance with applicable laws and regulations. Employment decisions are based on qualifications, experience, performance, and role requirements, without regard to any characteristic protected by applicable law.

Recruitment Agencies

Recruitment agencies and search firms must obtain prior written approval from ShoreVest's Human Resources team and agree to applicable terms before submitting candidate information. ShoreVest does not accept responsibility for unsolicited submissions or related fees where no prior written agreement exists.

Recruitment Fraud Notice

Candidates should remain alert to fraudulent emails, messages, or social media communications claiming to represent ShoreVest. Official recruitment communications take place only through recognized ShoreVest channels, and ShoreVest will never request payment as part of the recruitment process. Candidates should not provide financial information, passwords, or sensitive identity documents in response to unverified communications, and should report suspicious messages through the contact information on ShoreVest's official website.
